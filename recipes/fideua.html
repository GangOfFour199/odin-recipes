Ingredients

  • Squid
  • Monkfish tail
  • 1/2 cup Tomato puree
  • 250g Fideua noodles
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • sweet Paprika
  • Spanish EVOO
  • 1 Onion
  • 12 jumbo Prawns
  • Parsley
  • Slices of Lemon
  • 1/2 teaspoon Saffron
  • 2 1/2 cups Fish broth

Method

  1. Add oil to a paella pan and cook squid for 4 minutes.
  2. Remove squid, then cook monkfish tail for 5 mintues. Add minced onion and garlic to pan.
  3. Slowly add the paprika and tomato puree, then shortly after add squid back to pan and mix all together.
  4. Add noodles and mix. Cook for a further 2 minutes.
  5. Evenly pour the fish broth into the pan, then once it boils slightly add the jumbo pranws.
  6. Allow to cook for 10 minutes, then drop temperature down to a simmer for 3 minutes.
  7. Take off heat and let the fideua rest for 5 mintues.
  8. Garnish and serve with fresh parsley and lemon slices.
