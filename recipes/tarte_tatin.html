Ingredients

  • 60g unsalted butter
  • 6 peeled Braeburn apples
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 300g all butter puff pastry
  • 30g melted, unsalted butter

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. On the hob, cook suagr and butter into a dark caramel.
  3. Cut apples into quarters.
  4. Neatly arrange apples in the basin, starting from edges to the centre. Brush with melted butter.
  5. Cook for 30 mins.
  6. Remove, place disc of pastry on top and tuck in edges.
  7. Cook in oven for further 40-45 mins so that pastry is golden and crisp.
  8. Allow to cool and serve apple side up.
